  • How to Play
  • 1. When everyone is ready, click Start a New Puzzle
  • 2. Start writing! Write down as many words as you can before time runs out. Be original!
  • 3. When time is up, players take turns reading out their word lists and work out their scores. Longer words are worth more points. Only words not written by another player count.
  1. Scoring
  2. 1. When time runs out, all players must stop writing. Each player in turn reads their list aloud.
    Important Any word that appears on more than one player's list must be crossed off all lists, and no one gets credit for it!
